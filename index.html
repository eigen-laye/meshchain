How it works

Buy Rare Wine
Earn $VIN

Consumers earn $VIN by “opening” their Digital Corks (1 Digital Cork = 1 real bottle) - this sends consumption data back to the winemaker.

Digitally Uncork Wine & Earn $VIN

Consumers earn additional $VIN by issuing Tasting Tokens (with $VIN attached) to up to 12 people sharing the same bottle - this provides customer acquisition for winemakers.

Share Wine & $VIN
& Buy Rare Wine with $VIN

The $VIN community grows, and valuable consumption and customer acquisition data is generated, every time a bottle is opened and shared.
